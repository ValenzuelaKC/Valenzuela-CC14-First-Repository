Why Human Rights are important?

Human rights are important because nobody should be abused or subjected to discrimination, and everyone should have the opportunity to discover their potential. Unfortunately, these fundamental rights and liberties are not enjoyed by a large number of individuals worldwide.

Human rights play a significant role in how individuals interact to one another at all societal levels including those of the family, in the community, the workplace, in classroom, politics, and international relations.Therefore, it is important that everyone work to understand what human rights are. Promoting justice and the welfare of society is made much easier for people when they have a greater understanding of human rights.
