Human Rights Consideration

Since the principle of human rights focuses on equality and fairness, everyone has the privilege to have it. Yet, there will be consequences that might occur, which will disrupt the pinnacle meaning of human rights. And that is when an individual’s right is being degraded which violates the principle of equality and fairness. As humans, we must take note that we should still consider others when making an action. As it might be good for yourself but harm others, which in an instance you yourself violate theirs.

Summarization Example of Human Rights

Nobody has the privilege to control your existence whereas nobody can also take it from you.
Nobody has the privilege to treat you like a guinea pig as a test subject unless it undergoes proper agreement as you’re willing on it.
Nobody has the privilege to discriminate against you as everybody must be treated the same, in particular the same conditions equally.
Nobody has the privilege to disturb your private space, including your personal belongings.
Nobody has the privilege to question your status whenever you take refuge as everyone is granted to be protected by the nation.
Nobody has the privilege to choose who will be your partner as well as having family with it other than yourself.
Nobody has the privilege to question your thoughts, religion, opinion, and expression as this will invalidate your own rights as a human.
Nobody has the privilege to invalidate whether what is your race, religion, culture, and so on and so forth that will stop you from being employed.
Nobody has the privilege to stop you from learning and reach your dreams to be successful.
Nobody has the privilege to interrogate the appropriateness of you having a proper standard of living.
